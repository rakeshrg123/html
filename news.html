Six new fatalities,861 new COVID-19 cases in country

India logged 861 new coronavirus infections taking the
total tally of COVID-19 cases to 4,30,36,132, while the active cases dipped to 11,058 ,
according to the union Health Ministry.

The death toll climbed to 5,21,691 with six fresh fatalities, the data updated at 8 AM stated.
The active cases comprise 0.03 per cent of the total infections, while the national COVID-19
recovery rate remained at 98.76 per cent, the ministry said.

A reduction of 74 cases has been recorded in the active COVID-19 caseload in a span of
24 hours.The daily positivity rate was recorded as 0.32 per cent and the weekly positivity rate
was 0.23 per cent, according to the ministry.

The number of people who have recuperated from the disease surged to 4,25,03,383, while the
case fatality rate was recorded as 1.21 per cent.The cumulative doses administered in
the country so far under the nationwide COVID-19 vaccination drive has exceeded
185.74 crore.
